Features

Everything you to know about the rover.

Chasis

Chassis is a major component of the rover. Its task is to keep all components together while driving and handling vertical and lateral loads caused by irregular terrain of martian surface.

Manipulator

The manipulator of is a 5 DOF robotic arm that provides 6 different controls to the base station astronaut. It demarcates structural design powered by two linear actuators and four servo motors for different controls.

Bio-Assembly

The Bio-Assembly will be functioning as a Mobile Science Laboratory to collect soil samples from designated sites and perform essential science evaluation of these samples using carefully designed tests and experiments.

Electronics

Electronics used are responsible for carrying out autonomous navigation and control, supplying power to the rover,carrying out communications, between the rover and the base station,and ensuring all other moduleswork in sync to complete the mission.
